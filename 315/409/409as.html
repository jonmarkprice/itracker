This homework problem is to be done individually, not in pairs or other groups. It is due by noon Thursday, 9 April.

Consider the hangman game. This is a modification of the game in your textbook at the end of chapter 8, mainly to make the JS unobtrusive and up to our standards. The game is fully functional, but is clunky to use. Modify the hangman page in the following ways:

  • Clear the old guess letter from the little text box beside the Guess button as soon as the guess is made, leaving the text box empty.
  • After every guess, return the focus to the little text box beside the Guess button. But not at the beginning or end of the game when it would be more appropriate for the focus to be on a button.
  • When the player loses, add a button to allow the player to see what the word was. Remove the button after use, and remove the word when play restarts.
  • The word “Guesses” should appear only after the second guess has been made. After the first letter is guessed, the word should appear as “Guess”, and at the beginning of the game, before the first letter is guessed, there should be no word.
  • Make all game play case insensitive, and make all guessed letters appear as lower case.
